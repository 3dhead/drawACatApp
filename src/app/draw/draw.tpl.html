Draw A Cat

Draw your own cat using your mouse or finger! Each part of the cat is drawn separately so that it can come to life when you're done. Follow the instructions below, and use the blueprint as a guide.

If your hand slips, you can undo the last line you drew by pressing the "undo" button in the top right of the canvas.

When you've drawn all the parts, you'll be able to save your cat and share it with the world. Have fun!
